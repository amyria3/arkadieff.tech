Many people in North America and Europe suffer from insomnia. The causes are diverse: from very simple things such as too much coffee (or too many conflicting activities) to bigger issues like stress, decease or medication - or even traumata and losses. When sleeping issues persist, despite the absence of the afore mentioned triggers, the medical field refers to this as primary insomnia. On the other hand, many people occasionally sleep poorly without being sick at all.
It fortunately happens to be the case that some methods of cognitive behavioral therapy for insomnia are useful for people in both groups. Based on this—and as my penultimate project at IronHack — I developed s concept for an App, including a prototype, which the end user can use for self-therapy or to improve his or her related to sleep. Since the effectiveness of online insomnia therapy in general has been proven, there is a high chance that my app would be effective if it were developed.
